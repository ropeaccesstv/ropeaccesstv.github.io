1. Show your face.

When looking for a new customer, the best thing you can do is introduce yourself as a professional in the business . But you must also know how to sell, because you cannot expect that the jobs will come without the least effort.

Every day you have to overcome a challenge, every time in a different way. Whether you make a phone call, or meet your customer in person, you must show that you are willing to put your face into everything you do.

And don't forget to give your business card.

3. Communicate clearly and directly.

Communication in the rope access business is of fundamental importance. If you communicate your message the wrong way, you won't get any job. Why should a prospect customer ask for your rope access services? What distinguishes you from your competitors?

An innovative way of communicating is to visually show how you do your job, since it is sometimes difficult to make people understand what a rope access worker does everyday.

A video ad, for example, has about fifty times more engagement than written text, and is way more persuasive.

Browse the RopeAccessTv catalog and show what you do during working hours. Your customer will finally understand how your job makes the difference.
