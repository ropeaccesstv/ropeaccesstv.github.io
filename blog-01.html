1. Forget the Flyers.

The good old times when a piece of paper was enough to advertise you are long gone. The truth is that the flyer you distributed within 5 miles went the same place as the promotional brochure of your local supermarket: it ended up in the garbage.

Nowadays you need something more innovative (and also less polluting) than plain and simple printed paper. If you've heard of:

  • Websites
  • email marketing
  • advertising videos

I recommend you try them as an alternative. Earth will be grateful to you.

2. Judge a Book from the Cover

The way you appear reveals a lot to your next customer, especially if he or she has the intention of starting a business with you. If at your first meeting you are:

  • rude and vulgar
  • dressed in rags and smelly

you will make a bad impression on this person and he - or she - is going to find someone else to work with.

I know what you are thinking:

"But I work in rope access, I could be covered with rubble all day!"

Well, you're right. But before meeting your next customer, consider going home and taking a shower. Or, (as I did), look for a business partner to discuss with potential customers for you.

3. Seize the fleeting moment.

"The best way to find customers is to wait for them outside the office."

In this blog I talk about digital strategies to improve your rope access business. But sometimes I have to say that the good old word of mouth and the door-to-door are still quite effective (but forget the flyers, as I said in point 1. Those do NOT work.).

Any social moment (and I mean any) is a potential business meeting. Here’s some ideas:

  • at the dentist
  • in a cafe
  • at a dinner with friends

Be polite and nice to everyone, because you never know who’s the person you are talking to.

4. Communicate what you sell.

Rope access is a business based on mutual trust between employer and worker, so if you want to sell confidence you must also communicate it. If you look like and underdog or a desperate, you will hardly communicate confidence to your possible customer..

But what can you do to communicate confidence? On the internet you can find hundreds of tutorials about NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), but I recommend it only if you really want to specialize in the art of selling.

For the rest you will only need three things:

  • be yourself, but not too much (you know what I mean)
  • take a "normal" attitude
  • behave in a socially acceptable way.

5. Image Is Queen, Video Is King

In my articles I told you about how video is essential for digital marketing. The best way to advertise yourself is to show how your rope access work solves customer problems. Fortunately, shooting a video is quite simple nowadays, if you follow these steps:

  1. buy a action cam and stick it on your helmet.
  2. start the recording and start working.
  3. finish working and stop the recording.

Wait a moment, it’s not over yet:

  1. turn on your computer for video editing capable of rendering video in 4k at 60 fps.
  2. open your favorite post production software.
  3. select the section of your video that you find interesting.
  4. apply color correction and noise reduction.
  5. export your video in a delivery codec suitable for social media.

If you can do all these things, good for you. But chances are, you don't have an editing workstation or technical video skills.
